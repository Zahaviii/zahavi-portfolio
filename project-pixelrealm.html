Pixel Realm

May 10, 2026
Godot Engine GDScript Pixel Art

Pixel Realm is a top-down pixel art RPG game built with Godot Engine. Inspired by classic retro RPGs like the early Final Fantasy and Dragon Quest series, the game aims to bring back the nostalgic feeling of old-school RPG adventures with a modern touch.

The game features a hand-crafted pixel art world complete with detailed characters, rich environments, and various enemies. Every asset in the game is carefully designed to maintain a consistent retro pixel art aesthetic that feels both classic and fresh.

Currently in early development, the movement system has been fully implemented, allowing the player to explore the world freely. The game world is designed with detailed pixel art tiles, trees, paths, and open fields that bring the environment to life.

Combat system is currently being developed, which will allow players to engage in battles with various enemies including slimes and other creatures. Enemy AI with respawn mechanics and a health bar system will also be added to create a more dynamic and challenging gameplay experience.

The long-term vision for Pixel Realm includes a full inventory system, quest system, NPC interactions, and an engaging storyline. The goal is to create an immersive RPG experience that captures the charm of classic pixel art games while delivering modern and polished gameplay mechanics.

Current Features:

  • Top-down movement system
  • Pixel art environment & tilemap
  • Player character
  • Enemy NPC (Slime)

Upcoming Features:

  • Combat system
  • Enemy respawn mechanic
  • Health bar system
  • Inventory system
  • Quest system
  • NPC interactions
  • Storyline & dialogue system
